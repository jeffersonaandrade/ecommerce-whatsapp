8. KNOWN LIMITATIONS & NOTES

  Image Processing:
    - HTTPS only (security requirement)
    - Limited to 5 extensions: jpg, jpeg, png, webp
    - Maximum 5 URLs per product (design choice)

  CSV Processing:
    - Maximum variations handled: unlimited
    - Variation types recognized: Tamanho (size), Cor (color)
    - Numeric separator: Both comma and dot supported

  Product Operations:
    - No bulk delete (only create/update)
    - Slug uniqueness enforced
    - SKU conflicts prevent cross-product reuse
